Accreditation

What Accreditation Means

Accreditation means that we have been assessed against internationally recognized standards and operate to the highest levels of quality and service. Being accredited provides you assurance that the certificates issued are credible, authentic, impartial, and issued after ensuring conformance with the specific standards.
The accreditation reduces risk for you and your customers, providing complete confidence that we have been evaluated as per international standards for our competency and performance capability.

The Accred Services International Board (Europe) – ASIB

The Accred Services International Board (Europe) Limited is a versatile accreditation organization offering exemplary service that matches the requirements of the certifier and the certified. We follow strict guidelines and adapt vital regulations aligned with world standards in accreditation processes.
ASIB is an independent organization engaged in offering accreditation and assessment services to certification bodies and training organizations. It aims to work in close coordination with similar accreditation bodies that plan, implement, and certify ISO standards globally.
Following global quality standards helps foster better trade relations and recognition among international business communities for mutual benefit.

Other Accreditations

Our collaborations ensure continuous improvement, transparency, and global trust in the certification process, enhancing credibility across all sectors.
