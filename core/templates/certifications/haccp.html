About

HACCP Certification (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point) is a globally recognized food safety approach designed to prevent microbiological, chemical, and physical contamination throughout the food production and supply chain.

This system works by identifying potential hazards, establishing critical control points, setting measurable limits, and ensuring that control procedures are properly validated, monitored, and maintained during operations.

Effective implementation of HACCP helps businesses protect and strengthen their brand reputation, increase consumer confidence, and comply with regulatory as well as market requirements.
