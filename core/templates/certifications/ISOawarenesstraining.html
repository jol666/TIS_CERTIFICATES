Why Should You Attend?

The ISO 37001 Awareness Training helps you understand the key principles of the Anti-Bribery Management System (ABMS) as outlined in ISO 37001. It provides essential knowledge on ABMS policies and procedures, the role of leadership in preventing bribery, internal audits, management review activities, and the importance of continual improvement within the organization.

Who Should Attend?

Ideal participants for ISO 37001 Awareness Training:

01

Beginners & Learners

Anyone who wants to understand the fundamentals of ISO 37001.

02

Compliance Aspirants

Professionals looking to gain practical knowledge of ABMS processes.

03

Career Seekers

Individuals aiming to build a career in Anti-Bribery Management Systems.

Learning Objectives

Key outcomes of ISO 37001 Awareness Training:

01

Foundation of ABMS

Gain knowledge of the core elements and principles of Anti-Bribery Management Systems.

02

Standards Integration

Understand how ISO 37001 connects with other ISO standards and legal compliance requirements.

03

Implementation Skills

Learn methods and techniques for effective implementation of ISO 37001 ABMS.

Educational Approach

The Trainer uses real-world examples during the sessions and encourages active participation through interactive Q&A. Practical classroom exercises are included to support better understanding and implementation of concepts. Additionally, the practice assessments follow the same structure as the final certification exam, helping participants prepare with confidence and familiarity.
