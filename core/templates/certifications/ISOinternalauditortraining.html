Why Should You Attend?

The ISO 37001 Internal Auditor Training helps you develop the skills required to perform internal audits based on the Anti-Bribery Management System (ISO 37001). It enables you to apply audit techniques within your organization to ensure compliance with anti-bribery requirements.

Through practical exercises, you will learn how to plan, conduct, and manage audit programs and audit teams effectively. Once you gain the necessary knowledge of ISO 37001 and auditing methods, you will take an exam. After passing the exam, you will be certified as an ISO 37001 Lead Auditor, proving your capability to perform professional audits using international best practices.

Who Should Attend?

Recommended participants for ISO 37001 Internal Auditor Training:

01

Compliance Representatives

Management representatives responsible for maintaining ISO 37001 compliance in the organization.

02

Department Heads

Leaders who oversee, verify, and monitor anti-bribery compliance across departments.

03

Profile Enhancers

Individuals looking to strengthen their professional profile with a globally valuable certification.

04

Knowledge Upgraders

Anyone wishing to expand their understanding of ISO 37001 and Anti-Bribery Management Systems.

Educational Approach

The training sessions are conducted using real-life examples supported by interactive questions and answers. Practical classroom exercises help participants understand concepts clearly through discussion. Additionally, the practice tests are designed to mirror the final certification exam format, providing a strong mock exam experience for better preparation.
