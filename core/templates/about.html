About Us

Building Trust Through Quality Certification and Global Compliance.

TIS Offer Management System Certification.

In today’s world, Companies, regardless of their domain, location or size, face an ever-increasing call for profitability and quality that can add to their sustainable progress. In order to turn these pressures into a competitive benefit, you need to develop an efficient management system personalized to your business processes and use it systematically to maintain and continually improve your company’s overall performance.

TIS Certification, is an independent Conformity Assessment body for Management system Certifications. We at TIS offer a broad portfolio of services within management system certification and related services. Through our progressive Risk-Based Certification approach, we evaluate your system’s compliance with international standards and measure how well key systems support your business processes while building sustainable business performance over time.

Our Mission is to provide courteous, friendly, ethical, “Adding-Value-Assessment”, certification services with integrity. We are a consumer-centric management systems certification body with mission, values and vision with purpose services that enhances operations and administration performance of client-organizations. Our prime motive is to provide cost effective certification services to our client base that adds value to and supports the concept of the continuous improvement of aspects of their business processes regardless of the size or location of the client.

Impartiality Statement

TIS understands the importance of impartiality and potential conflicts of interests in carrying out its management system certification activities. TIS commits that all its certification activities are undertaken impartially, and the maintenance of impartiality is a key priority at all levels within TIS.

TIS applies its policies and procedures in a non-discriminatory way, and its services are available to anyone who seeks certification activities that are within its defined scope of activities. TIS certification decisions are based on objective evidence of conformity, or nonconformity, and are not influenced by other interests or by other parties.

The impartiality and objectivity of the certification services that TIS provides is tightly controlled. All TIS internal and external personnel are required to commit to confidentiality and impartiality, and are required to declare any potential conflicts of interest.

TIS does not provide internal audits to its certified clients, nor does it certify a management system on which it provided internal audits or consultancy in the past two years.

TIS evaluates potential risks to its impartiality on an on-going basis through internal audits and management reviews, and by consultation with interested parties. Where risks are identified, TIS uses appropriate measures to eliminate or minimize them.

Customers or interested parties who have concerns regarding TIS impartiality may use our complaint and appeal procedures for resolution.
